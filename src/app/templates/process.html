The Process

Pre - Work

After TechWise Course 1, we had a month break before this project officially began. During that time, we explored and researched our areas of iterest in relation to this project.

July

Project 1 started on July 6, 2022. Throughout the first couple weeks, we spent time diving into our areas of interest in relation to this project. Progress was fast and slow at the same time. We found success quickly using code that worked, but the process of rewriting our code to combine with the rest of the group was slow and challenging.

August

When August hit, our progress increased. Once our code was combined efficiently and we finished the last couple scenes, we succeeded in meeting our MVP (Minimal Viable Product).
